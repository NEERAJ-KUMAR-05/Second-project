ADVANTAGES

Voice assistants provide a range of advantages for users, including personalization, efficiency, multitasking, accessibility and hands-free control of smart home devices. We can use them anywhere. Additionally, they can be used to control things in your home, making everyday tasks easier and more convenient.

DIS-ADVANTAGES

Voice assistent can sometimes misunderstand what you say. They are always listening, which can be a privacy concern, and they need a good internet connection to work. There is also a risk that hackers could use them to access personal information or control smart devices.

SCOPE

Technological advances are making voice assistants more capable, particularly in AI, natural language processing (NLP), and machine language. To build a robust speech recognition experience. The artificial intelligence behind it must become batter at handling challenges such as accents and background noise.
